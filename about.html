About JC Elite Equine

Developing Tomorrow’s Grand Prix Partners

Founded in September 2024, JC Elite Equine blends boutique attention with international reach. We specialise in sourcing raw, high-potential prospects and guiding them through every stage of development until they are confident, competitive partners for the jumper ring.

  • Hands-on programs designed for long-term soundness and mental confidence.
  • Transparent updates, video, and vet insight shared throughout production.
  • Concierge-level trial coordination for riders travelling nationally or abroad.

What Sets Us Apart

Boutique Attention with International Reach

Intelligent Sourcing

We maintain close relationships with European and Canadian breeders, attending selections in person to evaluate talent, temperament, and rideability before horses enter our program.

Purposeful Development

Thoughtful training blocks, expert flatwork, and targeted show schedules build confidence without shortcuts, producing horses that stay rideable under pressure.

Concierge Client Care

From tailored trial itineraries to seamless import logistics, we curate every detail so riders and owners can focus on finding the right partner.

Training Philosophy

Intentional Development, One Horse at a Time

Each horse in our care follows an individualized plan that balances education, conditioning, and recovery. We partner with trusted veterinarians, bodywork specialists, and riders to ensure every step of the process supports long-term soundness and confidence.

  • Hand-selected youngsters from elite breeders across Europe and Canada
  • Classical flatwork, gymnastics, and cross-training to build rideability
  • Programs designed around FEI calendars, client timelines, and long-term goals

Our Approach

From Prospect to Podium

A structured pathway ensures every horse we represent is matched to the right rider, with full transparency from first ride to final handshake.

01

Source & Assess

We identify promising youngsters with the scope, carefulness, and temperament to excel. Each horse receives a comprehensive vetting and baseline training plan before entering our program.

02

Develop & Showcase

Thoughtful flatwork, careful gymnastic schools, and strategic showing build confidence and exposure. Clients receive regular updates, video, and performance insights.

03

Match & Support

Trial experiences are curated to each rider’s needs, with complete dossiers, logistical support, and post-sale follow-up to ensure a seamless transition.
