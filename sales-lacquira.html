Lacquira Z

2020 | Mare | 16.1 hh

Located in Langley, BC — trials available by appointment

Overview

Lacquira Z is a modern, athletic young mare with the quality and character to develop into a top show jumper. Sired by Lamborghini Z and out of a Quinar Z mare, she carries proven European bloodlines along with a generous temperament. Carefully produced within the JC Elite Equine program since 2024, she combines scope, carefulness, and rideability that make her an exciting investment for a rider aiming at the Young Horse classes and beyond.

She made a confident show debut at Thunderbird Show Park in September 2025, stepping into the 0.90m divisions during her first week out and handling the venue with composure well beyond her experience. Brave, intelligent, and naturally forward-thinking, she remains easy to adjust in a simple snaffle with no special prep.

At home she continues to build balance and adjustability through pole work, gymnastic lines, and varied conditioning. She hacks quietly and recently enjoyed a trail ride through Campbell Valley Park, demonstrating her steady brain outside the ring.
