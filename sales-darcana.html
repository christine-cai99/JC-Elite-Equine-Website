Darcana Z

2020 | Mare | 17 hh

Located in Langley, BC — trial rides available by appointment

Overview

Darcana Z is a 2020 Zangersheide mare by Darcon vh Kunselhof Z (Darco) and out of Chimana Z. She is a beautifully modern young horse with exceptional breeding, movement, and temperament.

Acquired in June 2025 as an unstarted five-year-old, Darcana Z has recently come under saddle and is progressing impressively fast. She shows a naturally rhythmic trot and canter, light balance, and a trainable, intelligent attitude that makes her a joy to develop.

This mare has a wonderful mind — sweet, brave, and easy to handle both on the ground and under saddle. She learns quickly and approaches new challenges with quiet confidence.

With her strong foundation, excellent pedigree, and willing nature, Darcana Z is offered in the mid to high five figures (CAD). Located in Langley, BC — trial rides available by appointment. Please contact JC Elite Equine Ltd. for inquiries.
